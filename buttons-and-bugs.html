Buttons and Bugs

Game Description

Not every hero starts at full-size. Some get shrunken. Others do the shrinking.
Buttons & Bugs is a pocket-sized adventure where strategy meets whimsy. Designed for quick quests and replayable delight, it shrinks epic decision-making into a world where every move matters—and every bug might bite back.
Perfect for newcomers and seasoned tacticians alike, this game invites you to: - Outsmart your opponent in bite-sized battles - Explore a world stitched from scraps and secrets
Play anywhere—on a train, at a tavern, or beneath a blanket fort It's cozy. It's clever. And it's crawling with charm.

Reviews

United Kingdom 4*

This is a very good solo game. I would say comparable to jaws of the lion. Parts ate obviously tiny but functional. Unfortunately I suffer with essential tremors so it is well fiddly for me. But as a genuine solo game I heartily recommend this affordable game that give you the gloomhaven jaws of the lion experience in a very compact size. You could even travel with just part of the set in a tiny card sized zip lock bag and play on your lunch break. I recommend

Germany 5*

It is a fantastic experience with perfect quality and Gloomhaven mechanics. We bought Gloomhaven Jaws of The Lion after that and started immediately cause we were aware of the gameplay from the B&B. Strongly recommend.

United States 5*

This is a delightful miniature version of the other big box games in the series. Small enough to bring through an airport and interesting enough to get you through a flight delay. It's an incredible way to spend some time not looking at a screen! The only thing I wish was different is that it was two player, but beggars can't be choosers. This game is an excellent value for the money. If you like the Gloomhaven franchise this is a must buy.
