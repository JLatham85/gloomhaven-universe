Gloomhaven

Game Description

Gold talks. Steel listens. And somewhere in the fog, fate takes notes.
Gloomhaven is a sprawling tactical campaign where mercenaries chase coin, clash with monsters, and accidentally become legends.
You'll crawl through crypts, bargain with demons, and retire just in time to open a bakery—or burn down a temple. No dice. Just decisions. No heroes. Just hired hands with questionable morals and excellent card decks.
Every choice reshapes the world. Every battle is a puzzle. And every unlocked character is a new flavor of chaos. Welcome to Gloomhaven. Wipe your boots. The city bites.

Germany 5*

The game is great in terms of scope, cards, rules, game principle and replay value. Players can quasi at any time or come in new, the legacy system could theoretically be circumvented if you want. The world map looks great and is quite easy to stick on. The short stories of the events are nicely described, the playable characters are great. The only thing that bothers me and “my party” are the mounts of the opponents/monsters these are now and then a little too thick for the plastic mounts with which you put them up so that the one or the other cardboard card at the corners frays. Definitely recommended is a organizing system there, once unpacked, not everything goes nicely organized back into the box! Absolute purchase recommendation if you have ever played a dungeon crawler.
