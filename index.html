Gloomhaven Universe

Why are we here ....

Some say this site was stitched together by candlelight and caffeine. Others claim it appeared one night, summoned by a bug-sized bard with a knack for naming scrolls.
Truth is—this is a portal.
A place where adventurers of all ages gather to explore the realms of Buttons and Bugs, Gloomhaven, Frosthaven, and the digital echoes of Gloomhaven Online.
It's where beginners book their first lessons, parties form in the shadows, and meetups spark new legends.
Whether you're here to learn, play, or simply wander through the codex, you've found the right scroll. The journey begins with curiosity—and ends wherever you decide to draw the map.

But what are these games? ....

It's not a video game. It's not a quiz. It's not a roll-the-dice-and-hope affair.
This is a tabletop campaign game—a genre where players gather around a table to explore maps, battle creatures, and shape a shared story. Think of it as part board game (with pieces, cards, and maps) and part roleplaying game (you become a character, grow stronger, and make choices that matter).
But unlike classic board games, there's no race to the finish. And unlike traditional RPGs, there's no game master or improv acting (although welcome at all times).
Instead:
- You play cards to move, attack, and use abilities—no dice involved.
- You plan each turn carefully, managing your hand like a spellbook.
- You unlock new content as you go—sealed envelopes, new characters, evolving maps. It's strategic.
It's story-driven. It's a slow-burning saga told in cardboard and courage.
